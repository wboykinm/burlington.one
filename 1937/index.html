Greater Burlington, 1937-2019

This map compares aerial views of the Burlington, VT metro area between 1937 and 2019, offering a window into patterns of residential and industrial development over nearly a century. Drag the slider to toggle between old and new.

  • 1937 Imagery acquired by the Sanborn Fire Insurance co. and archived by the UVM Howe Library
  • 2019 Imagery by the Vermont Center for Geographic Information (VCGI)
  • Historical imagery georeferenced and hosted using Mapwarper, by the NY Public Library
